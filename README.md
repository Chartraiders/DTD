## Game of Skill — The Compliance Foundation

Chart Raiders is a peer-to-peer Game of Skill contest platform. Day Trading Decrypted is the public, verifiable foundation of that classification.

**What makes competitive trading a Game of Skill:**

- **Multiple decisions per match** — players make many analytical decisions over each match duration. The aggregate of skill-based decisions determines final rank — not a single market movement.
- **Standardized starting conditions** — all players begin with the same fictional bankroll ($100). Real-world wealth confers no advantage.
- **Measurable skill progression** — Day Trading Decrypted develops and rewards the exact competencies that determine match outcomes. A player who completes the curriculum performs better in competition over time.
- **Consistent skill advantage** — skilled players statistically outperform less skilled players across many matches. This is the defining characteristic of skill-based competition.
- **Rank-based payouts** — prizes are distributed by player rank among peers. Payouts are not tied to the direction or value of any underlying asset.

Players never buy, sell, or hold any securities. Day Trading Decrypted teaches analysis of publicly available market data within a fictional competitive environment.
